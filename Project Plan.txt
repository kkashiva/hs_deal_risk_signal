Deal Risk Early Detection & Intervention 
Problem
By the time a deal is marked Closed Lost, intervention is no longer possible. Risk signals exist earlier in the sales cycle but are scattered across calls, notes, deal properties, and activity data, making them hard to detect consistently.
Proposed Solution
Build an AI-assisted system that continuously analyzes open deals to identify early risk signals and recommend corrective action before deals are lost.
The system would aggregate signals such as:
Gong call transcripts and recent meeting notes (Matrix, manual AE written)
Deal stage duration, amount changes, and forecast category updates vs average for each AE
Sales activity frequency (meetings, follow-ups, no-shows)
Public data (company signals eg. funding, hiring, layoffs, etc.)
Using an AI-based analysis (with potential upskilling into LangChain for chaining prompts and managing memory of past outcomes):
Flag deals as low / medium / high risk
Identify primary risk drivers (eg. budget, timing, champion, competition)
Provide supporting evidence from calls or activity 
Recommend specific next actions for AEs (or managers / Jonathan to step in case needed)
Example Outputs
“Deal Risk Level” and “Primary Risk Reason” fields
Short AI-generated explanation with evidence
Suggested intervention (task, manager review, enablement prompt)


Business Impact
Improved win rates through timely intervention
Better forecast reliability and deal inspection
Flag high risk deals to manager/Jonathan on live dashboard. With recommended action


System Design (v1)
A. Daily Scheduled Risk Scan

Run via Vercel Cron once per day.

Step 1 – Fetch Open Deals

From HubSpot:

Pipeline = X

Stage not Closed Won/Lost

Stage age

Forecast category

Amount history

Last activity date

Owner

Close date drift

Step 2 – Fetch Gong Data

Per deal:

Last 1–2 transcripts

Optional: AI summary first (separate prompt for cost control)

Step 3 – Build Risk Input Object

Structured JSON input to LLM:

{
  "deal_metadata": {...},
  "engagement_metrics": {...},
  "transcript_summary": "...",
  "activity_summary": "...",
  "historical_changes": {...}
}
4️⃣ Prompt Strategy (Critical)

You need structured output, not essays.

Risk Classification Prompt

Use a strict schema request:

{
  "risk_level": "LOW | MEDIUM | HIGH",
  "primary_risk_reason": "budget | timing | no_champion | competition | feature_gap | low_engagement | multithreading_gap",
  "explanation": "max 4 sentences",
  "recommended_action": "specific action",
  "confidence_score": 0-100
}

Force JSON output.

Do not allow free-form output.
